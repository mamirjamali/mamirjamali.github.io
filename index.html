Amir Jamali

Software engineer
Experienced in data science

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering, but eventually, I realized that I lacked passion for this field. Instead, I had a strong interest in computers. Consequently, I started taking various computer-related courses. I began by pursuing Microsoft Certified IT Professional courses in Tehran Institute of Technology, focusing on areas such as Network+, Active Directory, and Network Infrastructure.

Building upon my background in MCITP, I developed an interest in programming and understanding its underlying structure. At this point, I felt that I might be ready to establish my own business. As a result, I launched several startups, including an Online Shop (gheymatshekan.com, 2013), Online Programming Tutorials (takamooz.com, 2014), and a Learning Management System (class724.ir, 2017), among others.

Following these experiences, I pursued a Master's degree in Information Technology Management - Ecommerce. During my studies, I discovered my passion for Machine Learning and Data Mining, which aligned perfectly with my enthusiasm for mathematics and statistics. During my exploration of Machine Learning, I became acquainted with Quantum Computing (QML) and its profound impact on our future.

In 2021 - 2022, I had the opportunity to work as a CTO at enviroble Ltd (UCL BaseKX - UK), where I honed my skills in utilizing machine learning techniques with large amounts of data.

I'm currently working as a software engineer at Pangian company(USA).

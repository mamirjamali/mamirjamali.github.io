Amir Jamali

Data Scientist/Fullstack Web Developer

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering, but eventually, I realized that I lacked passion for this field. Instead, I had a strong interest in computers. Consequently, I started taking various computer-related courses. I began by pursuing Microsoft Certified IT Professional 2008, focusing on areas such as Network++, Active Directory, and Network Infrastructure. Although it was a challenging endeavor, I persevered and successfully completed the certification.

Building upon my background in MCITP, I developed an interest in programming and understanding its underlying structure. At this point, I felt that I might be ready to establish my own business. As a result, I launched several startups, including an Online Shop (gheymatshekan.com), Online Programming Tutorials (takamooz.com), and a Learning Management System (class724.ir), among others.

Following these experiences, I pursued a Master's degree in Information Technology Management - Ecommerce. During my studies, I discovered my passion for Machine Learning and Data Mining, which aligned perfectly with my enthusiasm for mathematics and statistics. During my exploration of Machine Learning, I became acquainted with Quantum Computing (QML) and its profound impact on our future.

In 2021 - 2022, I had the opportunity to work as a CTO at enviroble Ltd, where I honed my skills in utilizing machine learning techniques with large amounts of data.
